Stella Fusaro

Hi, I’m Stella — a filmmaker, designer, and computer science graduate based in New York. My work lives at the intersection of visual storytelling and technical precision. Whether I’m editing a short film, designing for impact, or building websites from scratch, I believe in creating work that is intentional, bold, and thoughtful.

I graduated from Columbia University with a degree in Computer Science, Math, and Film. My experience spans directing and editing short films, crafting creative video media for advertisements, and coding dynamic, responsive websites (like this one!).

I also program for WKCR-FM, where you can hear me on Wednesdays from 3–6pm hosting a show dedicated to film scores — with a focus on marginalized composers, including women and people of color. When I can, I also love curating classical music programming.

Outside of this work, I spend a lot of time in the ballet world — both dancing and watching. I’m a member of the Junior Council at the American Ballet Theatre, and I love choreographing pieces whenever I can. I also enjoy planning events, and I’m a certified bartender from the Columbia School of Mixology. Crafting cocktails is one of my favorite creative outlets.
