Films

Goodbye, Scruffy (May 2025)

Written, directed, and edited by me, this is one of my first narrative films with dialogue. It follows three women as they bury something—what exactly is left unsaid.

In a Moment (June 2023)

My very first film: a dance piece inspired by Maya Deren’s magical realism. Shot and edited in a single day, it explores a girl’s internal escape from the claustrophobic pressures of men’s voices through a dancer in her mind.

Let the Children Burn the Bodies (November 2023)

A creative documentary capturing Guy Fawkes Night in the English village of Crondal. Through a surreal lens, it reflects on the strange tradition of children burning effigies—an oddly normalized ritual.

Inclusus (September 2024)

An experimental art film designed to disorient. Two girls find themselves trapped in an eerie, undefined space. Nothing is explained—and that’s the point.

Isolation: A Video Essay (December 2024)

My first video essay, created entirely without narration or text. Through imagery alone, it explores themes of isolation in the work of filmmaker Melina León.
