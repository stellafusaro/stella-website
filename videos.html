Advertising & Promotional Videos

This is a selection of promotional and branded video work I’ve produced, directed, and edited. Each piece reflects a different approach to storytelling — whether through montage, archival footage, or stylized commercial structure. These videos were created to spark interest, build community, or elevate identity through moving images.

Morningside Art Exchange: Yankee by MAE Hat (May 2025)

I produced, co-wrote, and co-edited this ad for a branded hat by MAE. I organized and scheduled every shoot over multiple days, cast participants, and developed the visual outline and montage edit before sound was added.

Freaknik (March 2025)

My first promotional video made entirely with archival footage, created for a Freaknik-inspired party at the Columbia Intercultural Resource Center. The goal was to foster space and community for students of color in the lead-up to our annual music concert.

Bubble & Bend (April 2025)

After seeing my Freaknik video, the Haitian and Caribbean student associations commissioned this promotional video for their traditional Wete Fete party. Made from archival footage, the video celebrates Caribbean pride and culture. While the party was ultimately canceled due to weather, the video was enthusiastically received.
